Informatics ushered into Medicine

Koichi Akashi

MEDICAL STUDENT • RESEARCHER • DEVELOPER

Koichi is a third-year medical student at the University of Manchester, pursuing Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBChB) degree. He graduated from the School of Medicine, University of St Andrews in July 2025 with Bachelor of Science Honours (Medicine). Along with his major in medicine he is a software developer and has designed & developed several systems in the past, notably ShukuYaku and ShukuYakuLAB. He is a member of multiple research projects led by a professor at Institute of Science Tokyo, and has published conference and journal papers, with 2 awards at the world conferences.

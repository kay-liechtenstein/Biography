Informatics ushered into Medicine


Born and grown up in Japan, Koichi is now a third-year medical student at the University of St Andrews, United Kingdom. Along with his major in medicine, he has extended his knowledge and experience in the field of informatics as a software developer in a couple of research teams led by figures in various universities, notably such as Institute of Science Tokyo. He is a scholar of both Funai Foundation of Information Technology and Japan Student Services Organization, and is passionate about algorithms, cybersecurity, machine learning, and their application to medicine. After graduation from St Andrews in the summer of 2025 with Bachelor of Science Honours (Medicine), he is expected to progress onto the School of Medical Sciences, University of Manchester for clinical training in pursuit of final MBChB (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery) degree planned to be conferred in 2028.
